Privacy Policy

Soonr is a dating app that lets friends help matchmake for you. This explains what personal data we collect, why, and what rights you have over it — whether you're a full member or just on the waitlist below.

What we collect

Profile information you give us — name, age, gender, bio, photos, prompts, education, job, location, and Instagram handle. Your matching activity — likes, passes, matches, and messages. Notes and recommendations from friends matchmaking on your behalf. And basic account information — your email address and authentication data.

Why we collect it

To run the core service: showing you (and any friends matchmaking for you) potential matches, enabling messaging between matches, and letting friends leave feedback or suggest prompts on your profile, with your knowledge and control over what's shown publicly.

Who we share it with

A small number of service providers run the app on our behalf: Supabase (database and authentication), Render (backend hosting), and Resend (transactional email, e.g. partner-link confirmations). We don't sell your data, and we don't share it with advertisers.

Your rights

From Settings, at any time, you can download a full export of everything tied to your account, permanently delete your account (removes your profile, photos, messages, matches, and all other personal data immediately — this cannot be undone), or deactivate your account temporarily (hides your profile without deleting anything, reversible by logging back in). If you're in the EU or UK, these rights are guaranteed under GDPR (Articles 15, 17, and 20).

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed immediately and permanently, aside from what's briefly retained in standard database backup/recovery windows.

Cookies & tracking

This site doesn't use cookies, analytics, or any tracking scripts. The only thing we store is what you explicitly submit through the waitlist form below.

If you're just on the waitlist

Before you ever create an account, all we hold is your email address, the time you signed up, and confirmation that you agreed to this policy. We use it only to email you when Soonr opens up — never for marketing, never shared with anyone else, never sold. Want off the list before you've ever signed up properly? Email us below and we'll delete it immediately.
