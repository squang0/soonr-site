How it works

You swipe. Your friends back you up.

Every profile on Soonr can have a matchmaker attached — someone who actually knows the person behind it. That's what makes the difference between a stranger's profile and an introduction.

STEP 1

Recruit your matchmakers

Invite the friends who already have opinions about your love life. They get their own matchmaker mode, separate from their dating profile.

STEP 2

They scout alongside you

Your matchmakers browse profiles too and shortlist people they think you'd genuinely click with — using judgment an algorithm doesn't have.

STEP 3

Every match comes recommended

Their picks land in your feed alongside the ones you find yourself — already vouched for by someone who knows you, not just an algorithm.
